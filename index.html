Some Questions About Our Products?

How do you ensure the freshness of the produce when shopping online?

We work closely with our suppliers to ensure that all fresh produce is delivered directly to our warehouse as soon as it’s harvested. Each item is carefully inspected and stored in optimal conditions. For delivery, we use refrigerated trucks to maintain freshness until it arrives at your door. We also offer same-day delivery for certain items to guarantee you get the freshest selection possible.

Can I select specific items of fresh food, or is it more of a random selection when I shop online?

You can absolutely choose the exact fresh food items you'd like to purchase! Our platform allows you to browse through categories like fruits, vegetables, meats, and dairy, where you can select your preferred items. You can even filter by freshness, organic options, and more, so you get exactly what you’re looking for.

What happens if the fresh food I ordered is not in the best condition when it arrives?

We take the quality of your order seriously. If any of your fresh food items arrive damaged or not up to standard, simply reach out to our customer service team, and we’ll either replace the item or offer a refund. We aim to make every experience as smooth and fresh as possible for our customers!
